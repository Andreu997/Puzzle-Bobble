Welcome to the Puzzle-Bobble wiki!

This game is being developed by the Brony Team, consisting of Carlos Martínez as the QA tester, Pau Olivé taking the role of main coder, Andreu Sacasas dealing with the management of the project and Marc Torres who takes care of art & design.

Puzzle Booble is an arcade puzzle video game in 2D for one or two players which consists in clearing levels and getting the higher score possible. For doing so, the player/s must control a device that launches diferent color balls (intended to be bubbles), and gather 3 or more of the same color.
